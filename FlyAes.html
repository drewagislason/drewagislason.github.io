A small implementation of the AES algorithm

This is an implementation of the AES algorithm, specifically ECB, CTR and CBC mode. Block size can be chosen in aes.h - available choices are AES128, AES192, AES256.

The implementation is verified against the test vectors in: National Institute of Standards and Technology Special Publication 800-38A 2001 ED
